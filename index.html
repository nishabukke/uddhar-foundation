about us

Welcome to the Uddhar Foundation aid network

Uddhar Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to building a better tomorrow through Health, Employment, and Education. We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and growth. Starting with a pilot project in Pune, we aim to create 1,000+ jobs, promote accessible healthcare, and strengthen quality education & digital literacy for underprivileged communities.

What we believe and What we strive for

vision

To empower communities by ensuring Health, Education, and Employment for all, while fostering dignity, sustainability, and self-reliance.

mission
  • Provide skill-based training & employment for youth and women.
  • Organize health initiatives & awareness programs for underserved communities.
  • Deliver quality education & digital literacy for children and young adults.
  • Expand impact through partnerships with government, corporates, and society.
  • Drive holistic development in areas of environment, women empowerment, sports, and social support.
